Learning To Type

I’ve been programming now for more than 5 years. I;ve developed my programming skills and knowledge nicely, but one thing I’ve never been happy with is my typing ability. While I’m not necessarily a hunt-and-peck typist, I am also not a confident typist. I ocassionally look down at the keyboard and am constantly fixing typos with the delete key. Overall, I would consider my typing very below average.

I’ve decided to change that. I’m taking an online typing course at TypyingWeb and will continue to practice my typing skills in the hopes of becoming comfortable with touch typing and eventually type at least 80 words per minute confidently.

During my research into finding a free/cheap online typing program, I ran across this article by Jeff Atwood titled, “We Are Typists First, Programmers Second,” which helped validate my resolution.
